# Privacy Policy — Tic Tac Toe: Gobble

**Last updated: March 29, 2026**

---

## Overview

Tic Tac Toe: Gobble ("the App") is a local strategy game. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, uses, and stores.

---

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App does not:
- Require an account or login
- Collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
- Track your location
- Access your camera, microphone, contacts, or photos
- Connect to the internet during gameplay
- Send any data to external servers

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

| Data | Purpose | Location |
|------|---------|----------|
| Haptics preference (on/off) | Remember your setting | Device storage |
| Colorblind mode preference (on/off) | Remember your setting | Device storage |
| Active skin selection | Remember your theme choice | Device storage |
| Tutorial completion status | Skip tutorial on relaunch | Device storage |
| Starting player preference | Remember your game setting | Device storage |

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

## In-App Purchases

The App offers optional cosmetic skin purchases through Apple's App Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. For details on how Apple handles purchase data, see [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

---

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

---

## Children

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. As the App does not collect data, material changes are unlikely.
